Hobbies

This page introduces Pinn's hobbies after class.

Art

I practice visual arts and digital painting because I enjoy exploring color, form, and mood. Working with images helps me slow down and notice small details in everyday life. I often start with simple sketches and build them up into finished pieces that express emotion, memory, or a moment I found interesting.

Sample Arts

Music

I compose music because I enjoy turning ideas and feelings into sound. I work with melodies, harmonies, and rhythms to create pieces that reflect moods or stories. Sometimes I start at the piano or guitar, other times I sketch patterns in music software and develop them with virtual instruments. Composing helps me think in new ways, and I like sharing drafts with friends for feedback before refining a piece into a finished track.
